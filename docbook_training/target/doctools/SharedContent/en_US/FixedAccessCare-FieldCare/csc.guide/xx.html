Support and contact information

The FTTx Field Tech Console is an Android™ application which allows field technicians to view the jobs assigned to them for the current day, to coordinate ONT provisioning with the installation, and to troubleshoot problems reported by the customer. Jobs can be assigned to field technicians through the CSC and they will then appear in the FTTx Field Tech Console. The application uses information from the CSC and the 5530 NA-F.

The FTTx Field Tech Console is part of the FTTx Support Template.

Motive's FTTx Support Template 2.0 integrates the 5530 Network Analyzer - Fiber (NA-F) with the Motive Customer Service Console (CSC) (which incorporates the Service Management Platform (SMP)). With this console, CSRs (customer support representatives) can troubleshoot, diagnose, and resolve optical access loop (such as GPON or FTTx) related issues as part of their problem-resolution flow. They can also assign jobs to field technicians, and the technicians can receive the jobs in the FTTx Field Tech Console.
